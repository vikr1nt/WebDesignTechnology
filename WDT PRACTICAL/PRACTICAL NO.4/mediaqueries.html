DUBAI

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates and the most populated of the country's seven emirates [5][6][7]. The city has a population of around 3.79 million (as of 2024) [8] more than 90% of which are expatriates. In the early 20th century, Dubai has been a center for regional and international trade, since the early 20th century, Dubai has been a center for regional and international trade, city, tourism, and luxury.[9] Beginning in the early 20th century, Dubai has been developed into a significant trade center for international and regional commerce and financial services.[11][12][13]. Dubai relies on revenues from trade, tourism, aviation, real estate, and oil production. However, oil contributes less than 1 percent to the emirate's GDP in 2018.[14]. In 2015, Dubai has the second-most-five-star hotels in the Arabian Peninsula, on the coast of the Persian Gulf.[15]. Dubai was the third most-visited city in the world.[16][17].
